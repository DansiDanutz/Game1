HOW TO PLAY

Goal: Divide the grid into rectangles so that:

  • Each rectangle encloses exactly one number.
  • Its cell area equals that number's value.
  • Rectangles never overlap and the whole grid is tiled.

📱 Drag & Draw: press a cell and drag to outline a rectangle.

❌ Tap to Clear: tap a drawn rectangle to delete it.
